Mission Trip Management,
All in One Place

Every tool a trip leader needs — from accepting the first donation to filing the last expense report.

Penny-Accurate Donation Tracking

When a donor gives to your trip, the software splits it across your team with zero rounding loss. Remainder pennies are distributed fairly. Every dollar is accounted for.

  • Credit/debit cards (2.9% + $0.30)
  • ACH bank transfers (0.8%, capped at $5)
  • Digital wallets via Stripe Link
  • Donors choose to cover processing fees
  • Real-time fee display when switching payment methods
  • CSV export with fees, net amounts, and check/cash tracking
💰

Task Management with Automated Reminders

Create tasks across 8 categories — preparation, training, documents, fundraising, logistics, packing, spiritual, and other. Assign to one person or the whole team at once.

  • Filter by status, category, or person
  • Track assigned date, due date, and completed date
  • Red overdue indicators so nothing slips
  • Daily email reminders for upcoming and overdue tasks
  • Weekly leader digest every Monday morning
✅

E-Signatures & Document Management

Upload liability waivers, medical forms, and trip agreements. Participants sign digitally right from the platform — no printing, scanning, or mailing required.

  • Built-in PDF viewer with digital signature capture
  • Guardian co-signatures for minors (via email link)
  • Full audit trail for legal compliance
  • 3-day advance reminders before deadlines
  • Automatic overdue notifications
  • Weekly leader summary of missing documents
📝

Fundraising Milestones & Progress

Set deadline-based milestones (like "50% raised by April 1") and instantly see who is on track. Per-person stoplight indicators make it obvious where to focus.

  • Green, yellow, and red status for each participant
  • Collapsible roster with individual progress bars
  • Summary line: "2 behind · 5 on track · 1 fully funded"
  • Self-funded exemption for participants not fundraising
  • Support staff excluded from per-person budget calculations
📈

Budget & Expense Tracking

Track every dollar in and every dollar out. One unified view shows Total Gross, Stripe Fees, Net Received, Checks & Cash, Expenses, and Available Balance.

  • Record expenses against funds raised
  • Net Received vs. Expenses = Available Balance
  • Support staff can view and record payments
  • Financial summary cards at a glance
💰

Donor Portal

Donors access their giving history through a simple magic link — no password, no account creation. They can download tax receipts, see trip updates, and view prayer requests.

  • Magic link authentication (no passwords)
  • Complete giving history with recipient details
  • Annual tax receipt download
  • Trip updates from the team they supported
  • Aggregated impact metrics
🙌

Trip Itinerary & Logistics

Build a day-by-day schedule the whole team can see. Add activities, locations, times, and descriptions. When plans change, update once and everyone is current.

  • Day-by-day itinerary builder
  • Copy activities from one day to another
  • Location and time tracking for each activity
  • Visible to all team members
📅

Team Discipleship

A mission trip is more than logistics. Assign reading plans, devotionals, and courses to your team. Track spiritual preparation alongside practical preparation.

  • Built-in reading plans (including mission-focused plans)
  • Leader can assign plans as team tasks
  • Track completion alongside trip preparation
  • Part of the larger MKDSCPLS discipleship platform
📖

Applications & Team Management

Customizable trip application form with optional auto-approval. Manage participant profiles, emergency contacts, trip roles, and more.

  • Public trip application page
  • Leader review and approval workflow
  • Application deadline enforcement
  • Participant profiles with phone, email, and emergency info
  • Support staff and self-funded role designations
