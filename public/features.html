Penny-Accurate Donation Tracking

When a donor gives to your trip, the software splits it across your team with zero rounding loss. Remainder pennies are distributed fairly. Every dollar is accounted for.

  • Credit/debit cards (2.9% + $0.30)
  • ACH bank transfers (0.8%, capped at $5)
  • Digital wallets via Stripe Link
  • Donors choose to cover processing fees
  • Real-time fee display when switching payment methods
  • CSV export with fees, net amounts, and check/cash tracking
💰

Task Management with Automated Reminders

Create tasks across 8 categories — preparation, training, documents, fundraising, logistics, packing, spiritual, and other. Assign to one person or the whole team at once.

  • Filter by status, category, or person

  • Red overdue indicators so nothing slips
  • Daily email reminders for upcoming and overdue tasks
  • Weekly leader digest every Monday morning

Feature FAQs

How do individual fundraising pages work?

Every team member gets a personal donation page with their own fundraising goal. Donors can give to a specific team member or to the trip as a whole. Individual gifts go to that person's goal; team-wide gifts get split automatically across members who are below their goal, penny-accurately. Leaders see per-person progress with stoplight indicators (green, yellow, red) and a summary line like "2 behind, 5 on track, 1 fully funded."

Can we use GO alongside Planning Center?

Yes. GO integrates with Planning Center Giving for fund mapping, donation sync, and reconciliation. Churches already running PCO for general giving can keep using it and layer GO on top for mission-specific tracking, participant-level fundraising, and trip logistics that PCO does not handle.

Is the free plan really free?

Yes. The Free plan covers up to 5 active trips with task checklists, manual payment tracking, and help center access with no time limit. Paid plans start at $9/month and add features like online donations, e-signatures, and larger storage. There are zero platform fees on donations on any plan.

How do e-signatures work?

E-signatures are built into the platform — no separate DocuSign or Adobe Sign subscription. Upload a PDF (waiver, medical form, trip agreement), assign it to participants, and the software captures a digital signature with a full audit trail. Minors can route to parents or guardians via email for co-signature. Starter plans include 1 signable document; Ministry plans include unlimited.

Can donors give without creating an account?

Yes. Donors never need an account to give. They can check out as a guest on the public fundraising page with card, ACH bank transfer, or digital wallet. If they want to see their giving history or download a tax receipt later, they request a magic link by email — no password, no account to create.

Does GO handle minors and guardian consent?

Yes. Minors on the team can have waivers and forms routed to a parent or guardian email address for co-signature. The platform collects consent with a full audit trail so your church has a legally defensible record. Emergency contact info and medical details are collected in the same flow.
