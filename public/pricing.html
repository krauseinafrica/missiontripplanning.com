What about payment processing fees?

GO charges zero platform fees on donations. You only pay Stripe's standard processing: 2.9% + $0.30 for cards, or 0.8% (capped at $5) for ACH bank transfers. Donors can choose to cover the fee so your team receives the full amount.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Start free, and when you need online donations or e-signatures, move to Starter or Ministry.

Is there a contract?

No contracts. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime. Yearly plans are billed annually with a 17% discount.
