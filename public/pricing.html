What You Actually Get on the Free Plan

Free is not a trial. It is a full working plan for churches running one or two trips a year.

Most "free" software is a time-limited trial or a stripped-down preview designed to push you to upgrade. GO's Free plan is a real plan. No credit card. No 30-day countdown. No locked features hidden behind a paywall you hit on day one.

On the Free plan you can create up to 5 active trips, build full task checklists across 8 categories, manage participants and applications, assign tasks to individuals or the whole team, track fundraising manually by recording cash and check payments, build day-by-day itineraries, and keep everything in 250 MB of storage per trip. The help center is open to you forever.

What the Free plan doesn't include: online donation processing, e-signatures, and automated reminders. If you are collecting checks by hand and printing waivers, the Free plan is enough. When you want Stripe donations and digital signatures, move up to Starter.

How We Keep It Free

Transparency on where the money comes from and where it doesn't.

We do not charge platform fees on donations. A lot of church software platforms tack on an extra 1-3% on top of Stripe fees — on a $100,000 trip fundraising year, that is $1,000-$3,000 taken out of the mission. We refuse to do that. Every dollar a donor gives minus the Stripe processing goes to your team. That is non-negotiable for us.

We use Stripe Connect. Donations go directly from the donor to your church's Stripe account — not through us. We never touch your donation money. This is more secure, cleaner for your accounting, and it means we cannot take a cut even if we wanted to.

Paid tiers fund the platform. Ministry plans at $39/month and Starter plans at $9/month pay for the servers, the developers, the customer support, and the features we keep adding. Churches that need more get more and pay for it. Churches that only need the basics pay nothing. That is the model.

Pricing FAQs

Are there platform fees on donations?

No. GO charges zero platform fees on donations on every plan — Free, Starter, and Ministry. You only pay Stripe's standard processing: 2.9% + $0.30 for cards, or 0.8% capped at $5 for ACH bank transfers. Donors can choose to cover the fee so your team receives the full amount.

Is there a free trial?

The Free plan is not a trial — it is free forever. It covers up to 5 active trips with task checklists, manual payment tracking, and help center access with no credit card required and no time limit. When you need online donations or e-signatures, you can upgrade to Starter ($9/month) or Ministry ($39/month).

What is included in the free plan?

Up to 5 active trips, basic task checklists, manual payment tracking (record cash and checks without online donations), 250 MB storage per trip, and help center access. Free plan users can manage participants, assign tasks, build itineraries, and track fundraising manually — enough for a church running one or two trips a year.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Start on Free, move to Starter when you want online donations, move to Ministry when you want unlimited trips and e-signatures. No contracts, no cancellation fees.

Are there church-size limits or participant caps?

No. Plans are limited by number of active trips and storage, not by church size or participant count. A 100-person church and a 10,000-person church pay the same price for the same plan. Unlimited participants per trip on all plans.

Do you offer nonprofit discounts?

Pricing is already structured for churches and ministries — there is no separate nonprofit discount because the Free plan covers most small churches and the paid plans are priced below comparable church software. If you are a church plant or a ministry with financial hardship, reach out and we will work something out.

Is there a contract or commitment?

No contracts. Monthly plans can be cancelled anytime. Yearly plans are billed annually with a 17% discount and can also be cancelled, though refunds on annual plans are prorated.
