Why Churches Need Mission Trip Management Software

Most churches manage mission trips with a patchwork of free tools that break down as trips grow.

If you've led a church mission trip, you know the drill: a shared Google Sheet tracking who turned in what, a group text nobody reads, and a Venmo chain that somehow loses $47 along the way. For a small team taking one trip a year, it works — barely.

But as trips grow — more participants, more donors, more documents, tighter deadlines — the cracks show fast. Fundraising gets messy when you can't track processing fees or split donations across the team. Documents get lost in email attachments. Communication breaks down when updates are buried in group texts. Money math doesn't add up when you're doing donation splits by hand.

Mission trip planning software solves all of this in one place — so your trip leaders can spend their time preparing the team spiritually instead of chasing down paperwork.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is mission trip planning software?

Mission trip planning software is a tool that helps churches manage every aspect of a mission trip in one place — online donations, team task management, document collection and e-signatures, fundraising tracking, budget and expense management, trip itineraries, and team communication. It replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, Venmo, email chains, and paper forms that most churches use.

How much does mission trip management software cost?

GO offers a free tier for churches getting started (up to 5 trips, basic task management). The Starter plan is $9/month and adds online donations with zero platform fees. The Ministry plan is $39/month with unlimited trips, e-signatures, and priority support. All plans charge zero platform fees on donations — you only pay standard Stripe processing.

Can donors give without creating an account?

Yes. Donors give directly on the trip's donation page — no account needed. They can choose card, ACH bank transfer, or digital wallet. After giving, they receive a receipt and can access a donor portal via magic link for giving history and tax receipts.

Does the software handle individual fundraising for team members?

Yes. Donors can give to specific team members or to the trip as a whole. Team donations split penny-accurately across all active fundraisers. Per-person milestone tracking with stoplight indicators (green, yellow, red) shows leaders exactly where the team stands.

Can we collect signed waivers through the software?

Yes. Upload liability waivers, medical forms, and other documents for digital signatures. Guardian co-signatures for minors happen via email link. Full audit trail for legal compliance, plus automatic reminders for approaching and overdue deadlines.
